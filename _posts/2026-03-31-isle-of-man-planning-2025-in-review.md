In 2025, **1,274 planning applications** were submitted to the Isle of Man government. This article pulls together the numbers using data from [PlanningPortal.im](https://planningportal.im), which covers 80,000+ planning decisions going back to 1984.

## Headline Numbers

| Metric | 2025 |
|  --|  |
| Total applications | 1,274 |
| Approved | 1,024 |
| Refused | 106 |
| Withdrawn | 30 |
| Decided | 1,160 |
| Approval rate | 88.3% |

## Year-on-Year

| Year | Applications | Decided | Approval Rate |

| 2020 | 1,606 | 1,597 | 87.9% |

| 2022 | 1,535 | 1,528 | 85.8% |
| 2023 | 1,782 | 1,760 | 88.5% |
| 2024 | 1,438 | 1,416 | 88.6% |
| 2025 | 1,274 | 1,160 | 88.3% |

2023 was the busiest year in the data at 1,782 applications. 2025 came in 29% lower.
